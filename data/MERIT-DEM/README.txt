The classified_slope_merit_dem_1km.tif in this directory was
generated from the MERIT DEM project data files.

download_all.sh will download the DEM GeoTIFF files and generate
matching slope files. To use download_all.sh, you must agree to
the terms in a Google Form linked from
http://hydro.iis.u-tokyo.ac.jp/~yamadai/MERIT_DEM/
(search for "Registration for Download" within the page).

Once downloaded, classify_slope.py will generate
classified_slope_merit_dem_1km.tif.
It takes approximately 9 hours.
